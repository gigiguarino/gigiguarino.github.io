Each of these games was made with Unity. The code is written in C#, and all of it can be found on my GitHub. All of the graphics for my games have been made in Pixen.

Please email me if you have found any glitches, or have any ideas for new games you'd like to see. This is my first time hosting my video games on the web, so I'm still learning! I am currently looking for new ways to host that run smoother.



To play the games: Clicking on the game titles will open up pages where you can play them.

The games are not yet available for play on mobile devices.

Flower Fall

This is the first game I've ever made. It was mostly just for me to test out Unity and see what I could do by making something really basic. All of the graphics were made by me.

Game rules:

  • Move your character with ARROW KEYS.
  • Collect flowers to boost your score.
  • Avoid skulls to stay alive.
  • Green flowers give you more health.



Tic Tac Toe

I created this game just to see if I could. It's a simple Tic Tac Toe with cutesy graphics. All of the graphics were made by me.

Game rules:

  • Should be played two player.
  • Each person takes turn clicking spots.



Battle Planet



This is the game I am the most proud of. I made this game at MHacks 7 with my boyfriend, Jason Grand. We completed the whole thing after working for about 30 hours straight. This game incorporates sounds, and multiple levels. Half of the graphics of this game were made by me, and half were made by Jason.

Game rules:

  • Move your character with ARROW KEYS.
  • Beam down to a planet with ENTER.
  • Shoot the aliens with the SPACEBAR.
  • The level is not over until you kill all spawned aliens and all caves.
  • To win the game you have to make it to the sun, and beat the final boss.
  • After beating a planet, the planet next to it becomes unlocked.
